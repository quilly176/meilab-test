Welcome to Mei Lab

The Mei lab investigates mechanisms of parental behavior in animal models. We aim to identify pathological factors underlying aberrant aggression that disrupts caregiving in parent–offspring relationships. Parental behavior is susceptible to a broad range of emotional and social influences. To address potential confounders, we examine relevant social behaviors, especially prosocial behaviors, that may share overlapping neural circuits. The comparative approach allows isolation of the neural circuits selectively involved in parental behavior. For more information, please refer to the Research Topics section.
